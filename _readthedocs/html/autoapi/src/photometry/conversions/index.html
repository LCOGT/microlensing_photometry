flux_to_mag(flux, flux_err, exp_time=None)[source]

Function to convert the flux of a star from its fitted PSF model and its uncertainty onto the magnitude scale.

Parameters:

  • flux (float) – Total star flux

  • flux_err (float) – Uncertainty in star flux

  • exp_time (float) – [optional] Exposure time in s

Input flux, flux_err can be scalar values or arrays. If an exposure time is given, the fluxes and uncertainties will be scaled by the exposure time.

Returns:

Parameters:

  • mag (float) – Measured star magnitude

  • flux_mag (float) – Uncertainty in measured magnitude

  • flux (float) – Total flux, scaled by the exposure time if given

  • flux_err (float) – Uncertainty on total flux, scaled by the exposure time, if given
